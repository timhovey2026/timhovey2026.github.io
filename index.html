The Plymbury Witch

A Novel by Kendra Vaughan

In the seaside town of Plymbury, where fog drifts in with the tide and secrets are passed down as carefully as heirlooms, magic lives quietly beneath the surface.

When long-buried truths begin to surface, one woman must reckon with the legacy she inherited and the power she never meant to claim. The Plymbury Witch is a story of memory, belonging, and the extraordinary hidden inside ordinary lives.
