The Plymbury Witch

A Novel by Kendra Vaughan

Nestled along the coast of Massachusetts sits the quaint town of Plymbury, steeped in history, tradition, and an undercurrent of magic.

Ravenna Greene is the latest to inherit the mystical Sea Glass Apothecary in this town where stars turned to glass and fell into the ocean. Local legend whispers that after centuries of tumbling, they’d be used to grant wishes by a witch with one green and one brown eye.

But when Pastor Chase Wilkinson arrives, he incites prejudice among the townspeople and a devastating fire that consumes the apothecary. With an unusable shop needing repair and nowhere else to go, Ravenna accepts a room in Chase’s church—with one caveat: no magic.

Now, caught between two worlds, Ravenna must navigate loyalty to her lineage and a growing attraction to Chase. Soon, she will face a heart-wrenching choice: love or magic.
